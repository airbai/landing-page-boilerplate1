1) Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how this app (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) handles information when you upload a photo to receive an AI-generated face rating. The app is designed for entertainment purposes only.

2) Information We Process

  • Photos you upload: images you choose to submit for AI analysis.
  • Generated results: outputs such as an AI face score or related feedback.
  • Basic app/diagnostic data (optional): limited technical information (e.g., crash logs) to help keep the app stable.

We do not require you to provide your real name, phone number, or account details unless the app explicitly asks for them.

3) How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze your photo and generate an AI face rating.
  • To display your results in the app.
  • To maintain and improve app performance (e.g., bug fixes, reliability).

Results are generated by AI and are intended for entertainment only. They should not be used for medical, legal, or other high-stakes decisions.

4) Photo Storage and Retention

We process photos only to provide the requested AI rating. Photos and generated results may be temporarily stored (for example, in secure processing or caching systems) to deliver the service and to maintain reliability.

We aim to retain photos and related outputs only as long as necessary to operate the app and provide the result, unless a longer period is required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

If the app includes an option to save results locally, that content is stored on your device under your control.

We do not sell your personal information.
